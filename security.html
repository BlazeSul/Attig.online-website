Security at Attiq

Last updated: 23 July 2026

Version 1.0

Attiq is built privacy-first. Your financial data lives on your device by default, we run no ads and no tracking, and where data is encrypted we tell you exactly how. This page describes our actual security model — not slogans.

The specifics

Encrypted backups — end-to-end

When you export a backup file, it is encrypted with AES-256-GCM. The key is derived from a password you choose using PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 with 210,000 iterations and a random 128-bit salt, with a random 96-bit nonce per backup. The password never leaves your device and is not stored anywhere — only you can decrypt the file. If you lose the password, no one (including us) can recover the backup. This is true end-to-end encryption.

App lock

You can lock the app behind your device biometrics (fingerprint / Face ID / iris) or device PIN, with a configurable auto-lock timeout. Sensitive screens are blurred in the app switcher. Biometric data never leaves your device.

Session & credential storage

If you sign in, your session tokens are stored in the platform secure enclave — iOS Keychain or Android Keystore — not in general app storage. Tokens are cleared on sign-out. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider and are never visible to us.

On-device receipt scanning

Receipt scanning runs entirely on your device using local text recognition (Google ML Kit). Receipt photos and their contents are never uploaded.

Bank connections

Bank connections, where available, use Australia's Consumer Data Right through Basiq, an accredited data recipient. You authorise access directly with your bank; your banking credentials are never entered into or visible to Attiq, and access is read-only. See our CDR Policy.

What we don't do

  • No advertising and no advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No selling or renting of your data — to anyone, ever.

Reporting a vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security issue, please email feedback@attiq.online with details and steps to reproduce. We appreciate responsible disclosure and will respond as quickly as we can.
