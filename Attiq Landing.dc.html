01 Multiple accounts

Every account,
one balance

Bank, savings, credit cards and cash — bring them all together. Attiq rolls them into a single net-worth figure so you always know where you really stand.

  • ✓ Unlimited accounts & account types
  • ✓ Combined total balance & net worth
  • ✓ Exclude any account from totals
02 Spending insights

See where your
money really goes

Every month Attiq breaks your spending down by category — so you can see at a glance that housing, food and shopping are the ones eating your budget, and by exactly how much.

  • ✓ Income, expenses & savings rate each month
  • ✓ Category breakdown with live percentages
  • ✓ Spending velocity & projected month total
03 Categories & subcategories

See exactly
where it goes

Every transaction is sorted into a category — then drill into subcategories to find the real culprit. Is it "Housing", or is it really the insurance renewal? Attiq shows you.

  • ✓ Smart auto-categorisation by merchant
  • ✓ Custom categories & subcategories
  • ✓ Tap any category to drill down
04 Budgets & goals

Set limits.
Hit targets.

Give each category a monthly budget and watch progress fill in real time. Set savings goals — an emergency fund, a holiday, a deposit — and track every dollar toward them.

  • ✓ Per-category monthly budgets
  • ✓ Savings goals with progress rings
  • ✓ Alerts when you're near a limit
05 Spending heatmaps

Spot your
patterns instantly

A colour-coded heatmap of the last six weeks reveals the days you spend most. See at a glance that Fridays are your weak spot — and Tuesdays your best.

  • ✓ Six-week daily spending heatmap
  • ✓ Average spend by day of week
  • ✓ Highest & lowest days called out
06 Receipt tracking

Snap it.
Sorted.

Photograph any receipt and Attiq's AI reads the merchant, amount, date and category — logging it as an expense automatically. No manual entry, no lost paper.

  • ✓ AI reads merchant, amount & date
  • ✓ Auto-categorised as an expense
  • ✓ Keeps a photo record for every purchase
07 Warranty tracking

Warranties that
remind you

Store proof of purchase for your appliances, tech and gear. Attiq counts down to expiry so you can claim before cover runs out — never lose a receipt again.

  • ✓ Track expiry dates with live countdowns
  • ✓ Organised by appliances, tech & more
  • ✓ Reminders before cover ends
08 Notifications & secure storage

Private by default.
Helpful by design.

Every transaction, balance and receipt lives on your device — nothing uploaded, sold, or shared. Meanwhile smart notifications nudge you about bills, budgets and goals.

  • ✓ Local device only — no cloud sync
  • ✓ No ads, no tracking, export anytime
  • ✓ Bill, budget & goal reminders
